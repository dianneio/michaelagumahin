Ten Stunning Places to Visit

BEACHES

BORACAY

A world-famous island known for its white sand beaches, clear waters, and vibrant nightlife.

KAWASAN FALLS

 A popular waterfall known for it's turquoise blue water and breathtaking scenery.

SIARGO

Siargao is known to be one of the famous places in the Philippines for surfing. Its also popular for its clear ocean.

 CORON ISLAND

Coron is known for its crystal-clear waters, stunning coral reefs, and World War II shipwrecks that attract divers from around the world.

Calle Crisologo street in Vigan City

The historic city of Vigan in the province of Ilocos Sur transports you to the 16th century with its old-world charm and beautifully preserved Spanish colonial architecture.

Intramuros old town in Manila City

The Intramuros wa build by

onquistador Miguel Lopez de Legazpi and until now its captivating ruins remain, offering visitors a glimpse into the grandeur of old Manila City. 

NATURE

MAYON VOLCAN

A popular tourist spot, it is renowned for its "perfect cone" because of its symmetric conical shape, and is regarded as sacred in Philippine mythology.

LOBOC ADVENTURE PARK

Loboc features activities like a zipline, a cable car, and exhilarating rides. While we can admire the 

 breathtaking panoramic views of dense forests and the serene Loboc river.


Chocolate Hills

The Chocolate Hills was believed to be formed from marine limestone creating a mesmerizing view of

over 1,200 cone-shaped hills that turn brown in the dry season, resembling chocolates.




Banaue Rice Terraces

It's Ancient hand-carved rice terraces, hailed as the "Eighth Wonder of the World."
